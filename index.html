    About Us

    A group of like-minded people joined hands towards the mission of Environment preservation, by growing up trees, plants, cleaning and restoring water bodies. The group was formed in August 2019, by Mr.Kasi Viswanathan, proprietor Chittal paper company, Vadapalani, who is noble hearted and relentlessly working towards the society since his very young age. With the able support of members who are all in the service of the society, for long time, in different areas including the service towards education of poor and underprivileged people, the trust has been built and growing big with more volunteers joining hands, towards the noble cause. We plant trees in areas where, its identified as deprived of trees or on demand basis, plant trees in private and public places, including Govt.Schools, religious institutions, gated communities, etc. All the works are towards nature and nature only, with appropriate permissions from all concerned. We are here to develop a pleasant natural habitat for all living beings on the earth without any kind of differences or conflicts with anyone/anything on it.

    Projects

    Chennai Middle School

    Cleaning up and Plantation in Chennai Middle School, Nungambakkam.

    Siruvapuri Temple

    With the proper permission from local and temple authorities the project of cleaning, deepening with jcb, and planting of trees around the pond was successfully completed, with the grace of the Almighty. Being the successful first project, under the trust, all other subsequent activities are being planned and executed with same grace till date.

    Thirukulam at Manapakkam was cleaned up and deepened based on the request of the locals and able support of other groups in the area.
